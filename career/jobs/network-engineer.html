Network engineer

Technology

Full-time

Remote

Sartor is seeking a Network Engineer to join its Information Technology team. The successful candidate will provide advanced-level design, implementation, and operations of Sartor LAN/WAN/Internet networking infrastructure

Requirements

  • 5+ years' experience providing network architecture and consulting
  • 3-5 year’s experience providing private/hybrid/public cloud, configurations, migrations, patterns, and practices.
  • Cloud networking architecture , design, patterns, and implementation
  • Excellent ability to multi-task by balancing multiple projects at the same time.
  • A self-starter demonstrating an ability to transition in technology, lead and guide architecture , and thoughtfully solve problems
