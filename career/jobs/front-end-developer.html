Front end developer

Technology

Full-time

Remote

We are looking for a technical leader to take the reins of these front-end experiences and embrace the opportunity to have a tremendous impact on both the product for our sellers, and on reporting capabilities across Sartor.

Requirements

  • Experience or a deep understanding of statically rendered websites, their benefits, and drawbacks
  • 5+ years of applied experience with modern SPA frameworks (React, Vue, Nuxt, etc.)
  • Variety of work. There is no typical day. You could be working on anything from migrations, production defects, designs, UX to delivering new features, product enhancements, better user experience or creating new services.
  • Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Engineering or business-related field
  • Ability to work with both technical and functional resources
  • Well organized and able to work independently
Apply now

Duties

  • Providing deep software development knowledge with respect to front end architecture, UX/UI design, and programming
  • Helping customers develop scalable applications with rich user experiences
  • Collaborate with designers early in the production process to help them understand tech capabilities
  • Leverage automation, cognitive and science-based techniques to manage data, predict scenarios and prescribe actions
  • Provide ongoing website analytics and reporting regarding usage and performance
  • Drive a culture of automation, test coverage and architect for Micro Services, API, Cloud Native and Headless Architecture - Decoupling the front ends and backends of the technology stack
  • Define and develop standards for UI Development
